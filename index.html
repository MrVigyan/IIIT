About Our Institute

IIIT Limited (National Institute of Information Technology) is an Indian multinational skills and talent development corporation headquartered in Gurgaon, India. The company was set up in 1995 to help the nascent IT industry overcome its human resource challenges. IIIT offers training and development to individuals, enterprises and institutions..
